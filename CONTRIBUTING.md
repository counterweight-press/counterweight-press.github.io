# Contributing to Counterweight

## What We Publish

- Counter-narratives grounded in verifiable evidence
- Stories that challenge comfortable national mythologies
- Pieces that surface structural truths obscured by dominant narratives

## What We Don't Publish

- Unsourced opinion
- Conspiracy theories
- Content targeting individuals rather than systems

## Submitting

If you have a sourced counter-narrative — a story that challenges a dominant national myth — get in touch via the anonymous email listed on the site.

## Article Format

Articles use Markdown with the following front matter:

```yaml
---
title: "Your Article Title"
subtitle: "A brief description"
kicker: "Category or label"
date: 2026-02-10
slug: your-article-slug
tldr: "A punchy 2-4 sentence summary."
colophon: >
  A note about the piece's editorial framing.
footnotes:
  - 'Footnote with <a href="#">source links</a>.'
---

Your content in Markdown...
```

## Formatting

```markdown
{:.first}
Opening paragraph gets a drop cap.

{:.section-break}
· · ·

{:.pullquote}
This text appears as a styled pull quote.
```

Footnote references in text:

```html
Some claim.<sup class="fn-ref"><a href="#fn1" id="ref1">1</a></sup>
```
